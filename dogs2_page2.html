Tips and Tricks for Training Your Dog

Congratulations on finding a new best friend and welcome to the "Tips and Tricks" page! Here, you'll find valuable information to help you train your dog. Let's get started!

Teaching "Sit"

  1. Hold a treat in front of your dog's nose.
  2. Move the treat upward, allowing your dog's head to follow, which will naturally cause the rear end to lower.
  3. As soon as your dog sits, say "Sit" and give them the treat.
  4. Repeat several times, gradually increasing the duration before rewarding.

Teaching "Stay"

  1. Ask your dog to sit.
  2. Extend your hand with your palm facing outward and say "Stay."
  3. Take a step back. If your dog stays, reward them. If not, return them to the sitting position and try again.
  4. Gradually increase the distance and duration of the stay.

Teaching "Come"

  1. Start in a controlled area.
  2. Call your dog's name followed by "Come."
  3. Reward your dog when they come to you, and give them plenty of praise.
  4. Practice in different environments to increase reliability.

Teaching "Shake Hands"

  1. Have your dog sit.
  2. Lift one of your dog's paws gently and say "Shake."
  3. Reward them with a treat when they give you their paw.
  4. Repeat until your dog understands the command.
